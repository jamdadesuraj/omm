About OMM Software

OMM Software Innovations Pvt Ltd was established in the year 2013 by a group of renowned IT professionals with a multifaceted hardware and software background focuses on engineering services and IT staffing solutions.

OMM Software has in depth expertise in developing IT Softwares and Real time embedded Software for various domains, including Multimedia, Automotive, Networking, Telecom, aerospace and defense, cloud, Wireless and education. We specialize in Web Design, Systems Design, Custom Software Development, Firmware, and Driver Development.

What We Do

We are devoted to providing excellent solutions that address your particular business requirements by leveraging the power of modern software technology. Led by a team of erudite professionals with hands-on experience in the IT industry, we strive relentlessly to help our clients boost their workplace productivity and reduce business costs. It results in gaining a competitive advantage in the industry. Being a leading software development and digital marketing company, we take it as our mission to help your business thrive. We provide cost-effective software solutions on diverse technological platforms and assist in making them successful.
